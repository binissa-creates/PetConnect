# Verdant Harmony Design System

## Brand & Style
Verdant Harmony is a design system rooted in organic stability and professional growth. The brand personality is grounded, reliable, and sophisticated, aiming to evoke a sense of calm focus and environmental connection. The visual style is "Corporate Naturalist"—combining the structure of modern corporate design with a palette and shape language inspired by nature. It features balanced proportions, breathable layouts, and soft, approachable forms.

## Colors
The color palette uses the `tonal_spot` variant to create a harmonic, nature-inspired environment in a `light` color mode.

*   **Primary:** #4a7c59 (Forest Green) — Used for primary actions and branding.
*   **Secondary:** #6b6358 (Warm Taupe) — Used for supporting elements and icons.
*   **Tertiary:** #c4a66a (Muted Gold) — Used for accents and callouts.
*   **Neutral:** #4a4e4a (Dark Sage) — Used for text and boundaries.

## Typography
The system utilizes **Public Sans** for all typographic needs to ensure a clean and neutral look.
*   **Headlines:** Bold and clear hierarchy (32px / 28px).
*   **Body:** Optimized for readability (16px / 14px).
*   **Labels:** Concise and legible (12px with 0.5px letter spacing).

## Layout & Spacing
A **fluid grid** system based on an **8px (Spacing 2)** rhythm is used. 
*   **Margins:** 24px.
*   **Gutters:** 16px.
The philosophy prioritizes whitespace to reduce cognitive load and enhance focus.

## Elevation & Depth
Hierarchy is established through **tonal layering**. Surfaces are stacked using subtle variations of the neutral palette. When shadows are necessary, they are highly diffused and tinted with the neutral color to maintain a soft, integrated appearance.

## Shapes
The system uses a **Rounded (Level 2)** shape language to reinforce the approachable brand personality.
*   **Small Elements (Buttons/Inputs):** 8px (0.5rem) radius.
*   **Medium Elements (Cards):** 16px (1rem) radius.
*   **Large Elements (Modals):** 24px (1.5rem) radius.

## Components
*   **Buttons:** Featured in Forest Green with 8px rounded corners.
*   **Cards:** Defined by 16px rounded corners and subtle tonal backgrounds.
*   **Inputs:** Clean borders with Primary color focus states.
*   **Chips:** Pill-shaped and used for status and filtering.
*   **Lists:** Minimalist with soft dividers to maintain a rhythmic vertical flow.
